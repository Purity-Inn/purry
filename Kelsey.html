Nyawira says even the greatest parents of them all don't have a manual when it comes to parenting and motherhood. "I always say it is God because you can only do much. I always say as a responsible mother I do what I can and leave the rest to God."

She says she loves the fact that she is a young mom adding that she wouldn't advice anyone to follow her steps but insisted on the benefits of having a child at a younger age.

"I can't tell you to get pregnant at 22 but if you happen to have a stable man and you have gotten to a point where you feel like life is good, sometimes I say yeah getting a kid at a young age it is a good thing.

She says at that age she was clueless and only God and life has taught her how to be a good mother.

"I got pregnant when I was in my third year of college. I was 22 years old when I got pregnant at 22 what do you know? Actually you know almost nothing in life. Umejua tu shule so you are just about to finish school ndio uingie into the real world," she said.

She added, At that particular point the day I did that pregnancy test and confirmed that I was pregnant I went and spent the whole day crying.
